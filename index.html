Navigating Digital Nomadland

    Using our tool, you can help make your boot camp goal a reality! Add up to three cities to your search (Be sure to click the "Add" button after you find your city!) and choose between four jobs in the tech sector for a boot camp graduate. Once you've selected at least one city (and no more than three!) and at least one job, click search. You'll see some charts generated comparing median salaries in your selected global markets, as well as quality of life information regarding each place organized into an accordion menu for you to browse. Happy visualizing!
